## Implementation Notes

- Both variants are implemented with a toggle button to switch between them
- Default variant: Worker-focused
- All text uses Indian English (e.g., "part-time workers" not "part time workers")
- Tone: Trustworthy, direct, and local
- Headlines are 6-8 words as specified
- Supporting lines are 10-14 words as specified

## To Switch Headlines

Edit `app/page.tsx` and replace the headline text in the respective variant section. All 3 options are provided above for easy copy-paste.
