# Security Policy for 2ndShift Platform

If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it to us at security@2ndshift.in

We take all security reports seriously and will respond within 48 hours.

# Scope
- Web application: https://2ndshift.in
- API endpoints: https://2ndshift.in/api/*
- Mobile applications (if applicable)

# Out of Scope
- Third-party services (Supabase, Razorpay, Google Analytics)
- Social engineering attacks
- Physical security issues

# Safe Harbor
We support responsible disclosure. Researchers who comply with this policy will not face legal action.

# Acknowledgments
We maintain a hall of fame for security researchers who help us improve our platform.

Thank you for helping keep 2ndShift and our users safe!
