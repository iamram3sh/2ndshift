## Presentation Tips

1. **Timing:** 10-12 minutes presentation + 5-8 minutes Q&A
2. **Focus:** Problem → Solution → Market → Traction → Ask
3. **Visuals:** Use charts, graphs, screenshots
4. **Demo:** Show live platform if possible
5. **Energy:** Be passionate about the problem
6. **Practice:** Rehearse multiple times
7. **Questions:** Prepare answers for common objections

**Common Questions to Prepare:**
- Why will companies switch from Upwork?
- How do you handle competition?
- What's your customer acquisition strategy?
- How do you ensure compliance accuracy?
- What's your exit strategy?
