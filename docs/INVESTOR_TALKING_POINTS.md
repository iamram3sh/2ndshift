### Q: Why will companies switch from Upwork/Fiverr?

**A:** Three reasons:
1. **Compliance risk** - Upwork/Fiverr don't handle Indian taxes. Companies risk ‚Çπ50L penalties for misclassification. We handle everything automatically.
2. **Lower fees** - We charge 5-12% vs their 20-30%
3. **Verified talent** - Our 3-tier verification ensures quality. Workers are background-checked, ID-verified, and payment-verified.

**Plus:** Companies already hiring in India want local support, UPI payments, and compliance. We're built for India, not adapted from US platforms.

---

### Q: How do you handle competition?

**A:** We have multiple moats:
1. **Compliance expertise** - Deep knowledge of Indian tax laws (hard to replicate)
2. **Network effects** - More users = better matches = more users (winner-takes-most)
3. **First mover advantage** - We're the first to solve compliance properly
4. **Trust system** - 3-tier verification creates switching costs
5. **India-first** - Built for local market, not adapted

**Competition:** Upwork/Fiverr ignore compliance. Local platforms lack scale and verification. We combine both.

### Q: How do you ensure compliance accuracy?

**A:** Multiple layers:

1. **Built-in compliance** - Every transaction automatically handles TDS/GST
2. **Tax expert advisors** - We have CA advisors reviewing our system
3. **Integration with tax systems** - Direct integration with GST portal

**Risk mitigation:** We're not a tax advisor - we're a platform. But we ensure all transactions are compliant by design.
