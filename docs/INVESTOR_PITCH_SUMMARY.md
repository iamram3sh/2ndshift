## ðŸ’¡ Pro Tips

### During the Pitch
1. **Start with the problem** - Make them feel the pain
2. **Show passion** - You're solving a real problem
3. **Use data** - Numbers are more convincing than words
4. **Tell stories** - Real examples resonate
5. **Be honest** - Acknowledge risks and challenges

### Handling Objections
1. **Listen first** - Understand their concern
2. **Acknowledge** - "That's a valid concern"
3. **Address directly** - Provide data/evidence
4. **Reframe** - Turn objection into opportunity
5. **Move forward** - Don't dwell on it

### Closing
1. **Ask for next steps** - "What would you need to move forward?"
2. **Set timeline** - "We're looking to close in [timeframe]"
3. **Create urgency** - "We have limited spots in this round"
4. **Be confident** - You're offering them an opportunity
